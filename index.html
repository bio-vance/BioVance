    • Product Overview

    BioVance OpticalCore is a revolutionary smart medical glass designed to assist doctors during surgeries and diagnostics with real-time data and augmented visuals.

    • Vital Signs Monitoring: Heart rate, BP, temperature
    • 3D Visual Graph: Micro-OLED projection of internal anatomy
    • Adjustable Power Lens: For vision correction
    • Inbuilt Flashlight: For low-light environments

    Advanced Features

    • Live Surgery Mode: AI alerts, overlays, auto vision adjustment
    • Training Sandbox: Practice cases, feedback, remote coaching
    • Device Control: Pairing, updates, fleet health checks
    • Integrations: EHR (FHIR), Imaging (DICOM)
    • Security: Role-based access, encryption, audit logs
